About me

Born and raised in northern California, I grew up on the slopes of Squaw Valley. From an early age you’d find me in the gym training for ski racing and playing on multiple sports teams, including club and ODP soccer teams. Even after moving on to play collegiate soccer at UC Santa Cruz, I still spent my winters competing on the Freeskiing World Tour and coaching the Squaw Valley Ski Team.

Oddly enough, it was an injury that made me realize my love for fitness. In 2014 while skiing with my sister, I broke my back and everything changed. Then began a period of intense self-rehab, bringing back my strength little by little, one day at a time. In the beginning, even lifting ten pounds was a struggle. Training and growing stronger each day, I worked to regain my strength and mobility. It was through this personal challenge that my passion for fitness came to the forefront of my life.

My passion for living an outdoor lifestyle and nourishing my body for longevity has a huge influence in my training. After rediscovering fitness and falling back in love with all the activities I grew up doing I decided to earn my certified personal training certification (ISSA) and then continued on to earn my Certified Functional Strength and Conditioning (CFSC) certification, EXOS fitness specialist certification (XFS), and became an indoor cycling instructor at Flywheel Sports.

Everything that I love to do is influenced by health and fitness, and because of this discovery that I have a new outlook on life. The stronger I am, the more confident I am everyday. I strive to help clients move past their own physical and mental limitations and achieve new fitness goals, to make fitness part of a lifestyle. By incorporating these changes you will see the biggest results in not only just your fitness journey but your life.

My Specialities

Functional Strength and Conditioning

For anyone trying to build a strong foundation, work on specific fitness goals, or rebuild after injury. If you have old injuries this is best for you, we will do correctional exercises mixed in with strength in a personalized program that will be tailored to maximize the potential of your body.

Box and Climb

Great for anyone who wants to learn boxing and get a great cardio workout in. We will head to the Santa Monica stairs for a head clearing, empowering hour of focus mitt training and booty blasting stair workouts.

Weight Loss

For anyone trying to shed a few extra pounds or is ready for a complete transformation. We will do a mix of strength and mobility mixed with a complete metabolic fat busting workout.

HIIT Training

For anyone that wants a serious butt kicking, high energy, interval training workout. Can be done in 1-1 training as well as group workouts.
