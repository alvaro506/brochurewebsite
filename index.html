What I do

Born and raised in Lake Tahoe California, I grew up living an active lifestyle with my family outdoors. Any given day you could find my training for soccer, ski racing, doing gymnastics, running track, water skiing, or mountain biking. With two older sisters on the US Ski team I was constantly chasing them and pushing my limits. I played soccer for UC Santa Cruz and competed on the Freeskiing World Tour until I moved to Los Angeles to write music.

After suffering a broken back in 2014 my passion for fitness was re-ignited and I decided to become a personal trainer and group fitness instructor.

My Specialities

Functional Strength and Conditioning

For anyone trying to build a strong foundation, work on specific fitness goals, or rebuild after injury. If you have old injuries this is best for you, we will do correctional exercises mixed in with strength in a personalized program that will be tailored to maximize the potential of your body.

Box and Climb

Great for anyone who wants to learn boxing and get a great cardio workout in. We will head to the Santa Monica stairs for a head clearing, empowering hour of focus mitt training and booty blasting stair workouts.

Weight Loss

For anyone trying to shed a few extra pounds or is ready for a complete transformation. We will do a mix of strength and mobility mixed with a complete metabolic fat busting workout.

HIIT Training

For anyone that wants a serious butt kicking, high energy, interval training workout. Can be done in 1-1 training as well as group workouts.

“Fitness isn’t only about the hours spent in the gym, it’s about the lifestyle you build around your workout routine, the other 23 hours in your day. My goal is to help each of my clients find the right balance that works for them, inside and outside of the gym.”
